Last updated: May 2026

1. Booking & Confirmation

A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of an advance payment (30–50% of total package cost). Until then, pricing and availability are subject to change. Confirmation will be sent via WhatsApp or email.

2. Payment Terms

The balance payment is due 48 hours before the trip start date. For bookings made within 48 hours of travel, full payment is required at the time of booking. We accept UPI, bank transfer and cash.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • 15+ days before departure: 90% refund of advance paid
  • 8–14 days before departure: 50% refund
  • 7 days or less: No refund

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days. Cancellation charges may vary for peak season bookings (Dec–Jan, summer holidays).

4. Changes & Amendments

Changes to travel dates or itinerary requested by the customer will be accommodated subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Please notify us at least 7 days before departure for any changes.
