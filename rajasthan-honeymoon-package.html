Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Romantic Arrival in Udaipur — Heritage Hotel with Lake View & Candlelight Dinner

Arrive at Udaipur Airport and be welcomed with a flower garland by our representative. Transfer to your carefully selected heritage hotel with views of Lake Pichola — perhaps a restored haveli or a boutique palace hotel in the old city. Your room is pre-decorated with marigolds, rose petals, and candles. After freshening up, enjoy a sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola — the Lake Palace Hotel shimmering on the water, the City Palace catching the last golden light, and the Aravalli hills turning purple in the dusk. A romantic dinner is arranged at a lakeside restaurant with soft music and candlelight.

  • Flower garland welcome at Udaipur airport
  • Heritage hotel check-in — Lake Pichola view, decorated room
  • Sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola
  • Candlelight dinner at lakeside restaurant; overnight
Day 2: Udaipur — City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon ki Bari & Rooftop Dinner

Spend the day exploring Udaipur's most romantic sights together. The City Palace is a 400-year layering of palaces built by successive Maharanas — the views from the top over Lake Pichola are breathtaking and perfect for couple photographs. Visit the ornate Jagdish Temple (17th century) with its magnificent carved elephant gateway. The Saheliyon ki Bari (Garden of the Maids of Honour) is a beautiful sunken garden with ornamental fountains, kiosks, and marble elephants — designed by Maharana Sangram Singh for the ladies of the royal household. In the evening, dine on a rooftop restaurant with a direct view of Lake Pichola illuminated at night.

  • City Palace — 400-year royal palace with lake panorama
  • Jagdish Temple — 17th century carved stone temple
  • Saheliyon ki Bari — ornate royal garden & fountains
  • Rooftop dinner with Lake Pichola night view; overnight
Day 3: Udaipur to Jaipur — via Pushkar Holy Town

Morning departure for Jaipur (6 hours) via the sacred town of Pushkar. Pushkar is one of India's holiest Hindu pilgrimage towns, centered on a sacred lake where pilgrims come to bathe and pray. The town is also home to the world's only Brahma Temple. Walk around the 52 ghats of Pushkar Lake — the atmosphere at sunrise, with priests performing rituals, devotees offering flowers, and bells ringing across the water, is profoundly atmospheric and deeply photogenic. Have lunch in Pushkar before continuing to Jaipur and check into your palace-style heritage hotel.

  • Morning departure Udaipur to Pushkar (3.5 hrs)
  • Pushkar Lake — sunrise rituals at sacred ghats
  • World's only Brahma Temple visit
  • Lunch in Pushkar
  • Continue to Jaipur (2.5 hrs); heritage haveli hotel check-in; overnight
Day 4: Jaipur — Amber Fort Private Jeep, City Palace & Rooftop Dinner

Private sightseeing in the Pink City. For a honeymoon experience, skip the elephant queue and opt for a private jeep ride to Amber Fort's entrance — faster and more exclusive. Spend extended time inside the Sheesh Mahal (Hall of Mirrors) — in candlelight, the mirror ceiling creates a magical starry effect. City Palace's Mubarak Mahal courtyard is beautiful for photographs. In the evening, Jaipur's rooftop restaurants serve excellent Rajasthani cuisine against a backdrop of the illuminated Nahargarh Fort on the hilltop above the city — an intensely romantic scene.

  • Amber Fort — private jeep ride (no elephant queue)
  • Sheesh Mahal — mirrored ceiling candlelight experience
  • City Palace — Mubarak Mahal courtyard photography
  • Hawa Mahal photography at sunset
  • Rooftop romantic dinner — views of illuminated Nahargarh Fort; overnight
Day 5: Jaipur to Pushkar — Brahma Temple Sunrise & Camel Photography

Rise early for a return day trip to Pushkar to experience the sunrise at Brahma Ghat — a truly otherworldly moment when the first light hits the lake and the golden temple spire. Try a camel ride around the desert outskirts of Pushkar for classic Rajasthan photography together. Browse the charming bazaars of Pushkar for Rajasthani silver jewellery, tie-dye fabrics, and the famous Pushkar rose water. Return to Jaipur by afternoon.

  • Early morning Pushkar day trip (2.5 hrs from Jaipur)
  • Brahma Ghat sunrise — golden light on temple & lake
  • Camel ride in Pushkar desert for couple photography
  • Pushkar bazaar — silver jewellery & tie-dye
  • Return to Jaipur; overnight
Day 6: Shopping & Departure from Jaipur

Final Rajasthani breakfast at the heritage hotel. Browse Jaipur's famous gem and jewellery markets for high-quality precious stones (Jaipur is India's gemstone capital), block-printed salwar suits, and blue pottery. Transfer to Jaipur International Airport for departure — carry the memories of royal Rajasthan and the beginning of your royal journey together.

  • Final breakfast and check-out
  • Johari Bazaar — gems, Kundan jewellery & block prints
  • Transfer to Jaipur airport; departure
