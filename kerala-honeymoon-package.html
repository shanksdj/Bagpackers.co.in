Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Romantic Arrival — Treehouse Check-in & Candlelight Dinner in Tea Gardens

Arrive at Cochin airport and be welcomed with fresh flowers. Your private car (not a shared cab) whisks you away to Munnar's most romantic accommodation — a treehouse resort perched among the tea gardens. Your room is decorated with tropical flowers, rose petals, and candles — a truly enchanting arrival. As evening falls, enjoy a private candlelight dinner either in the treehouse or at a candlelit spot within the tea garden, with the fireflies beginning to glow and the mountain silence creating perfect intimacy.

  • Flower welcome at Cochin airport
  • Private car transfer to Munnar (3 hrs)
  • Treehouse resort check-in — flower-decorated room
  • Private candlelight dinner in tea garden setting
  • Overnight treehouse
Day 2: Munnar Tea Estate Private Tour & Romantic Boat Ride on Kundala Lake

After a leisurely breakfast, enjoy a private guided tour of the tea estate — walk through the tea rows, learn how leaves are hand-picked and processed, and sample freshly brewed estate teas. Drive to Echo Point on Mattupetty Lake and then to the beautiful Kundala Lake for a private boat ride — the couple-only paddleboat or rowboat on the lake with tea-covered hills as a backdrop is absolutely stunning. Photography is spectacular here. Evening in the tea garden for a private romantic setup — your choice of a candlelit terrace dinner or a cozy in-room arrangement.

  • Private tea estate guided walk & tasting
  • Echo Point — shout & hear your voices echo
  • Kundala Lake — private boat ride for two
  • Photography in tea gardens
  • Romantic evening dinner; overnight
Day 3: Munnar to Alleppey — Premium AC Houseboat Couple Cruise

Check out of the treehouse and drive to Alleppey (4 hours) through rubber plantations and backwater country. Board your premium air-conditioned houseboat — this is a step above the standard houseboat, with a private bedroom featuring a king bed, a private bathroom, a living room, and a large sun deck. Cast off into the Vembanad backwaters as your personal chef prepares a delicious Kerala feast. Watch the golden sunset from the sun deck — the sky turns extraordinary shades of orange and pink over the mirror-calm water. Stargazing from the sun deck after dinner is a highlight of the honeymoon.

  • Drive Munnar to Alleppey (4 hrs)
  • Premium AC houseboat check-in — private king bedroom
  • Afternoon backwater cruise — villages, paddy fields, birds
  • All meals prepared by onboard chef
  • Sunset & stargazing from sun deck; overnight houseboat
Day 4: Morning Cruise — Transfer to Kovalam & Ayurvedic Massage

Wake to birdsong and rippling water for a final morning on the backwaters. Houseboat check-out at 9 AM. Drive 3 hours to Kovalam Beach and check into your sea-facing beach resort. After lunch, enjoy a 1-hour couples Ayurvedic massage at the resort spa — Kerala is the home of Ayurveda, and a traditional warm oil massage here by trained therapists is deeply restorative. In the evening, visit the Kovalam Lighthouse Beach and watch the sunset from the promontory — one of India's most beautiful sunset spots.

  • Morning backwater cruise; houseboat check-out 9 AM
  • Private car to Kovalam (3 hrs)
  • Sea-facing beach resort check-in
  • 1-hour couples Ayurvedic massage at resort spa
  • Lighthouse Beach sunset walk; overnight Kovalam
Day 5: Kovalam Beach Leisure, Sunset Kayaking & Romantic Dinner

Your last full day in Kerala is all yours. Spend the morning swimming in Kovalam's gentle waves, sunbathing on the crescent beach, or attending the resort's morning yoga class on the beach. In the late afternoon, try sunset kayaking — paddle through the calm coastal waters as the sun descends, with the lighthouse framed in golden light. Your evening romantic dinner is arranged at a specially selected open-air seafood restaurant on the beach — fresh lobster, Kerala prawns, and fish molee with the sound of the waves as your soundtrack.

  • Morning beach leisure — swimming, sunbathing or yoga
  • Late afternoon sunset kayaking
  • Romantic beach dinner — fresh seafood & Kerala cuisine
  • Overnight Kovalam
Day 6: Breakfast & Departure from Trivandrum

Final breakfast at the beach resort. Transfer to Trivandrum International Airport (45 minutes). Kerala's God's Own Country has given you a honeymoon of unforgettable natural beauty and romance.

  • Final breakfast and resort check-out
  • Transfer to Trivandrum airport (45 min)
  • Departure
