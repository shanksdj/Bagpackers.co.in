Package Overview

South Goa is a world apart from the busy north — quieter beaches, richer history, and one of India's most spectacular waterfalls. This 3-night, 4-day South Goa Serenity Package takes you through the UNESCO-listed Old Goa churches, the charming Portuguese quarter of Fontainhas, the pristine shores of Palolem and Colva, and the breathtaking Dudhsagar Falls deep inside Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary. Priced at ₹8,499 per person, this package is ideal for nature lovers, history enthusiasts, and anyone who prefers Goa's quieter side.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Evening at Palolem Beach

Upon arriving at Goa's Dabolim Airport or Madgaon Railway Station, our driver will welcome you and transfer you to your hotel near the tranquil Palolem or Colva Beach area. Palolem is widely considered one of India's most beautiful crescent-shaped beaches, and your first evening here sets the perfect tone for this serenity-focused trip. Take a leisurely stroll along the soft white sands, dip your toes in the gentle waves, and watch the sun descend into the Arabian Sea in a blaze of crimson. Many small beach shacks serve delicious fresh seafood — try the Goan fish curry rice as your first dinner in Goa.

  • Arrival transfer from airport or Madgaon Railway Station
  • Hotel check-in at Palolem or Colva beach area
  • Evening walk and sunset at Palolem Beach
  • Dinner at leisure at beach shack (Goan cuisine recommended)
  • Overnight at hotel
Day 2: South Goa Sightseeing — Churches, Jetty & Fontainhas

Today is a rich cultural and heritage day. Start with Miramar Beach, Goa's nearest beach to Panjim — less crowded and serene in the mornings. Move to Dona Paula Jetty, a historic promontory where two rivers meet the sea, offering beautiful views of the Zuari estuary. The highlight of the day is the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Old Goa — walk through the massive Basilica of Bom Jesus, which houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, and the towering Se Cathedral, once the largest church in Asia. Finally, wander through Fontainhas, Panjim's Latin Quarter — a narrow network of Portuguese-era pastel houses with terracotta roof tiles, tiny cafes, and art galleries.

  • Miramar Beach — quiet morning walk
  • Dona Paula Jetty — scenic river-sea confluence
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus — UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Se Cathedral — largest church in Old Goa
  • Fontainhas Latin Quarter — colonial architecture stroll
  • Overnight at hotel
Day 3: Dudhsagar Falls Jeep Safari & Spice Plantation

Today is the adventure highlight of the trip. Start early for the Dudhsagar Falls excursion — Goa's most spectacular waterfall, plunging 310 metres in a milky white cascade (the name means 'sea of milk'). The only way to reach the falls is by open jeep safari through Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary's dense forest trails — thrilling in itself, with chances of spotting wildlife. You'll wade through a shallow stream to reach the base of the falls for a refreshing dip and stunning photos. On the way back, stop at a working Spice Plantation for a guided walk through vanilla, pepper, cardamom, and nutmeg groves, followed by a traditional Goan lunch served on banana leaves (included). End the day with a relaxed evening at Colva Beach.

  • Early departure for Dudhsagar Falls (jeep safari through wildlife sanctuary)
  • Swim and photography at base of Dudhsagar Falls
  • Spice Plantation guided tour — vanilla, pepper, cardamom
  • Traditional Goan lunch at spice plantation (included)
  • Evening at Colva Beach — sunset stroll
  • Overnight at hotel
Day 4: Palolem Leisure & Departure

Enjoy your final breakfast in Goa at a leisurely pace. Spend the morning at Palolem Beach — this is a perfect opportunity for a kayak ride, a yoga session on the beach, or simply sitting with a book watching the fishing boats bob in the calm bay. Many travellers pick up spices, handmade jewellery, or hammocks from the small stalls lining the beach path. As your departure time nears, our driver will transfer you comfortably to Dabolim Airport or Madgaon Railway Station.

  • Final breakfast and hotel check-out
  • Leisure morning at Palolem Beach (kayaking/yoga optional)
  • Departure transfer to airport or railway station
