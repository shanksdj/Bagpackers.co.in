Package Overview

Experience the best of Goa with this carefully crafted 3-night, 4-day beach holiday package. From the vibrant shores of Calangute and Baga to the historic ramparts of Fort Aguada, this trip covers everything a first-time Goa visitor needs. You'll enjoy exciting water sports, witness the magic of a dinner cruise on the Arabian Sea, and soak in the legendary Goan nightlife — all at an unbeatable price of ₹7,999 per person.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Beach Evening at Calangute/Baga

Arrive at Goa's Dabolim Airport or the Madgaon/Thivim Railway Station and be greeted by our representative. Transfer to your hotel for a smooth check-in. After freshening up, head to the iconic Calangute Beach — Goa's largest beach — or the lively Baga Beach just a kilometre away. Enjoy the warm golden sands, grab a refreshing coconut water, and watch the setting sun paint the horizon orange and pink. Your evening welcome drink is on us. Return to the hotel for a good night's rest in preparation for the adventures ahead.

  • Airport/railway station pickup and hotel check-in
  • Evening at Calangute or Baga Beach (your choice)
  • Welcome drink served at hotel
  • Overnight at hotel in Calangute/Baga area
Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing — Forts, Markets & Beaches

After breakfast, set out for a comprehensive North Goa sightseeing tour by private cab. Your first stop is Calangute Beach for a quick photo session before moving to the famous Anjuna Flea Market (Wednesday market) — a treasure trove of handicrafts, jewellery, clothes, and local spices. Next, visit the majestic Fort Aguada, a 17th-century Portuguese fortress perched above the Arabian Sea offering breathtaking panoramic views. Continue to Chapora Fort, made famous by the Bollywood film Dil Chahta Hai, where you'll enjoy sweeping views of the Vagator coastline. The evening is free for leisure, personal shopping, or simply lounging at the beach.

  • Calangute Beach & Baga Beach visit
  • Anjuna Flea Market — handicrafts & souvenirs
  • Fort Aguada — 17th century Portuguese fort with sea views
  • Chapora Fort — iconic viewpoint above Vagator
  • Evening at leisure; overnight at hotel
Day 3: Water Sports at Baga & Dinner Cruise Party

This is the day you've been waiting for! Head to Baga Beach in the morning for a thrilling water sports session. Your combo package includes a heart-pumping Jet Ski ride (15 minutes), the fun-filled Banana Boat Ride, soaring high above the sea on a Parasailing adventure, and a fast-paced Speed Boat ride — all with experienced instructors to ensure your safety. In the evening, board a magnificent Dinner Cruise on the Mandovi River. Dance to live music, enjoy Goan and Indian cuisine served on board, watch traditional folk performances, and cruise along the glittering Panjim waterfront as the city lights reflect on the water. This is an evening you will never forget.

  • Jet Ski ride (15 minutes) at Baga Beach
  • Banana Boat Ride for the whole group
  • Parasailing — fly high above the Arabian Sea
  • Speed Boat ride along the Goa coastline
  • Evening Dinner Cruise on Mandovi River with live music, dance & Goan cuisine
Day 4: Panjim Shopping & Departure

Enjoy your last breakfast in Goa and check out of the hotel. Head to the colourful Panjim (Panaji) market — Goa's capital is famous for its old Portuguese-style houses in the Fontainhas neighbourhood, the vibrant Mapusa or Panaji market for cashews, feni, spices, and pottery. Pick up the perfect souvenirs for your friends and family back home. As departure time nears, our team will transfer you to the airport or railway station in a comfortable, air-conditioned cab. Carry back golden memories of your Goa beach holiday!

  • Breakfast at hotel and smooth check-out
  • Shopping at Panjim market — cashews, feni, spices, pottery
  • Departure transfer to Goa airport or railway station
