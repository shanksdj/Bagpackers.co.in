Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bagpackers.co.in ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website or contact us about tour packages.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp, phone, email or web form, we may collect: your name, phone number, email address, destination preferences, travel dates and group details. We do not collect payment card numbers or sensitive financial data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to: provide tour quotations and booking confirmations, communicate trip details and itineraries, send service updates relevant to your booking, and improve our services. We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

3. WhatsApp Communication

When you initiate contact via WhatsApp, you consent to receive messages from us related to your booking and travel queries. You can opt out at any time by requesting us to stop messaging you.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Your data is accessible only to Bagpackers staff involved in processing your booking.

5. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies for functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at info@bagpackers.co.in.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at info@bagpackers.co.in or WhatsApp +91-9810467420.
