    FAQ

    What is included in the watersports combo?
    The watersports combo includes Parasailing, Jet Ski Ride, Speed Boat Ride, Banana Ride, and Bumper Ride.
    Do I need prior experience for scuba diving?
    No prior experience is required. A 20-minute pre-dive training session with a certified trainer is provided. No Requirement of Swimming also.
    Are meals provided during the tour?
    Yes, Day 2 includes a Veg/Non-Veg lunch with soft drinks, and a dinner buffet on the cruise.
    Is transportation included in the package?
    Yes, free pickup and drop-off are provided from your hotel for all activities and sightseeing.
    What attractions are covered in the South Goa sightseeing tour?
    South Goa tour covers Balaji Temple, Panjim Church, Se Cathedral Church, Manguishi Temple, Miramar Beach, and Dauna Paula Beach. You can enjoy serene locations and historical sites..
    What attractions are included in the North Goa sightseeing tour?
    North Goa tour includes Snow Park, Sinquerium Beach, Calangute Beach, Candolim Beach, Baga Beach, Vagator Beach, and Aguada Fort. It provides a mix of vibrant beaches and historical landmarks.
    What should I bring for the trip?
    Bring comfortable clothing, swimwear, sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat. All safety equipment for activities is provided.
    Is the dinner buffet on the cruise suitable for vegetarians?
    Yes, the dinner buffet includes both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to all dietary preferences.
    What activities are available during the dinner cruise?
    The dinner cruise includes DJ music, live entertainment, games with prizes, scenic views, and access to the AC dance floor.
    Can children join the dinner cruise?
    Yes, the dinner cruise is family-friendly, and children are welcome to enjoy the experience.
    Can Non-Swimmers do scuba diving?
    Frankly, there is no correlation between swimming and scuba diving. Swimming is the act of keeping ourselves above the water while scuba diving is done under the water than technically we don't need to know how to swim. Scuba diving does not require the use of the arms, just the fins (feet)
    What is the minimum age for scuba diving?
    The min. age is 14 years for scuba diving with us for complete safety purposes.
    What is the difference between a private tour and a group tour?
    Private Tour: Offers exclusive transportation and a personalized itinerary for you and your group, providing flexibility and privacy. Group Tour: Includes shared transportation and activities with other travelers, offering a more social and budget-friendly experience.
