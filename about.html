FAQ

What is the best time to visit Goa?
The ideal time to visit Goa is from November to February when the weather is pleasant and cool, perfect for beach activities and sightseeing. This period also includes the vibrant Goa Carnival and the popular Sunburn Festival.
Are the tour packages customizable?
Absolutely! We understand that every traveler has unique preferences. Our Goa tour packages are fully customizable, allowing you to tailor your itinerary to suit your interests, whether it's adding more beach days, cultural tours, or adventure activities.
What kind of accommodation options are available?
Our packages offer a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious beach resorts. We ensure that all accommodations are comfortable, safe, and provide excellent service.
Are there family-friendly activities included in the packages?
Certainly! Goa is a great destination for families. Our packages can include family-friendly activities like dolphin watching, visits to wildlife sanctuaries, and fun at water parks. We ensure activities are enjoyable for all ages.
Is scuba diving available in Goa?
Diving is conducted by PADI certified scuba diving centers in Goa. You can explore underwater sea life the best way by opting for this package. Scuba diving places in India that offer Goa Scuba diving packages are Malvan, Goa (Grande Island).
Which beach is best for scuba diving in Goa?
Grande Island, also know by other names like Bat Island, Illa Grand, Grande Island is the best place for scuba diving in Goa.
What are the must-visit places in Goa?
Goa is filled with must-visit places, including the beaches of Calangute and Anjuna, the historical Fort Aguada, the UNESCO World Heritage Churches in Old Goa, and the spice plantations in Ponda. Our packages aim to cover a mix of popular and offbeat destinations.
Which is the best beach in Goa for water sports?
Calangute Beach. Goa has plenty to offer in terms of adventures and experiences, including water sports.

  • Dona Paula
  • Anjuna Beach

Is scuba diving an expensive water sport?
No not at all It is a very nominal cost watersport with best excitement and adventure.
Is Scuba diving dangerous?
According to statistics Scuba diving is one of the safest sports if you keep yourself within the PADI guidelines taught to you.
What is there to do in Goa in January?
Many parties at Baga, Candolim, Calangute, Anjuna, and the nightclubs throughout Goa. There are beach parties as well. Plenty of water sports activities to choose from, including scuba diving, snorkeling, and fishing. The beach shacks are all open, serving their food and drinks
