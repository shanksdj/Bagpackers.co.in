About Bagpackers

Your Travel, Our Passion

Who We Are

Bagpackers is India's trusted multi-destination travel company, founded with a simple belief: every Indian deserves an extraordinary holiday without breaking the bank.

We specialize in crafting hand-picked tour packages for five of India's most beloved destinations — Goa, Manali, Shimla, Kerala, and Rajasthan — covering beach escapes, mountain adventures, backwater retreats, royal heritage tours, and romantic honeymoons.

From our Delhi headquarters, our team of dedicated travel experts handles everything — hotel bookings, transport, sightseeing, activities, and 24/7 WhatsApp support — so you can focus on making memories.
