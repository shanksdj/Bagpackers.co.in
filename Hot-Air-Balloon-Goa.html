    FAQ's

    How to reach the starting location?
    The boarding point for this hot air balloon in Goa flight is Assolda, a football ground in Quepem Taluk in South Goa and the location is easily reachable by public and private transport. Report at 6:30 AM, 15 minutes before the departure time for pre-flight briefing, take off at 6:45 am.
    What are the best clothes I can wear for balloon ride?
    There is almost no difference in the temperature inside the balloon and the ground level. So, depending on the temperature, you can choose what to wear. But it is conventional to wear casual and comfortable clothes. It is advised to wear good fitting shoes and carry sunglasses, hat, jacket.
    What is the maximum height that the balloon travels?
    The hot air balloon can take you as high as 2500 feet. But depending upon the air limit and weather conditions, this limit may vary.
    Is there any age restriction?
    Yes. Children below the age of 5 are not allowed. Also, for children below the age of 9, the fare is 50% that of an adult.
    How can I trust the safety of the balloon ride?
    The agency that controls and restricts the flying of the airplanes, DGCA, is subject to control hot air balloons as well. And the pilots are well trained and have years of experience. The Tiger Safaris is in operation for 10 years and it’s running smoothly since then with trained pilots and staff.
    What is the maximum limit of the flight distance?
    The balloon travels approximately 15 - 20Km . Sometimes it can go more than that. But the hot air balloon is subject to weather condition hence, it may vary as well. The altitude of the balloon varies as well.
    * Please carry your mask, Sanitizer,towel, cap, sunglass, suncream etc
