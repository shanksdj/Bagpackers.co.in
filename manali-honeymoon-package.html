Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Romantic Arrival — Private Cottage & Candlelight Welcome Dinner

Arrive in Manali and be welcomed with fresh flowers by our representative. Transfer to your private cottage or boutique mountain resort with stunning views of snow-capped peaks. Your room has been pre-decorated with roses, flower petals, and aromatic candles. As evening falls, enjoy a private candlelight welcome dinner — either at the resort's garden or by a crackling fireplace inside the cottage, with a curated menu of continental and Indian dishes. This is the beginning of your Himalayan love story.

  • Couple welcome with flowers at arrival point
  • Transfer to private cottage/boutique resort (mountain view)
  • Room decorated with roses, petals & candles
  • Candlelight welcome dinner — fireplace or garden setting
  • Overnight at cottage
Day 2: Hadimba Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs & Romantic Walk on Mall Road

After a leisurely breakfast, visit the ancient Hadimba Devi Temple — its setting in a towering deodar forest is incredibly romantic and makes for beautiful couple photographs. Head to Vashisht village for a couples hot spring soak — the warm sulphur water is therapeutic and the small village cafe nearby serves excellent Himachali tea. Visit the Tibetan Monastery for some quiet time together among the prayer flags. In the evening, stroll hand-in-hand along Mall Road and choose from several romantic restaurants for a private candlelight dinner.

  • Hadimba Temple — deodar forest couple photography
  • Vashisht Hot Springs — couples therapeutic soak
  • Tibetan Monastery & Old Manali stroll
  • Evening candlelight dinner on Mall Road
  • Overnight
Day 3: Solang Valley Snow Activities & Private Snow Photography Session

Head to Solang Valley for a snow-filled romantic day. Activities include zorbing (side by side in separate balls), snow scooter rides, skiing lessons (seasonal), and an exhilarating cable car ride with 360-degree mountain views. The highlight is a private photography session in the snow — our partnered photographer will capture stunning couple shots against the dramatic backdrop of snow-covered Himalayan peaks. Return in the evening for a cosy bonfire and BBQ dinner at the resort.

  • Solang Valley — zorbing, snow scooter & activities
  • Cable car panoramic mountain views
  • Private couple photography session in snow
  • Evening bonfire & BBQ dinner at resort
  • Overnight
Day 4: Beas River Rafting & Couples Spa Session

Today combines adventure with indulgence. Start with white-water river rafting on the Beas River — the Grade II–III rapids are thrilling but manageable for beginners, and the emerald river valley is beautiful. After the rafting high, unwind with a 60-minute couples Ayurvedic spa session — traditional warm oil massages that leave you both completely relaxed. In the afternoon, drive to Rohtang Point (seasonal, permit required) for breathtaking snow views. Evening at leisure for a romantic dinner of your choice.

  • Beas River rafting — Grade II-III rapids (14 km stretch)
  • 60-minute couples Ayurvedic spa session
  • Optional Rohtang Pass drive (seasonal)
  • Romantic dinner at your choice of restaurant
  • Overnight
Day 5: Shopping & Departure

Enjoy your last breakfast with Himalayan views. Explore Manali's local markets for Kullu shawls, Himachali caps, dried apricots, walnuts, and local honey — perfect souvenirs. Our driver will transfer you to the Volvo bus stand or Bhuntar Airport at your scheduled time.

  • Final breakfast and hotel check-out
  • Shopping — Kullu shawls, dry fruits, local honey
  • Departure transfer to Volvo stand or Bhuntar Airport
