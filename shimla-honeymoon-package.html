Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Romantic Arrival — Heritage Hotel, Fireplace Dinner & Decorated Room

Arrive in Shimla (Volvo from Delhi, morning arrival) and be welcomed at the station with a fresh flower bouquet. Transfer to your carefully selected heritage boutique hotel — a colonial-era property with character, charm, and mountain views. Your room has been pre-decorated with flowers, petals, and candles. After freshening up, enjoy welcome drinks on the hotel terrace with Himalayan views. In the evening, a romantic dinner by the fireplace in the hotel dining room — the classic combination of mountain cold, warm fireplace, and candlelight is utterly romantic.

  • Flower welcome at Shimla station
  • Heritage boutique hotel check-in (mountain/valley views)
  • Room decorated with flowers, petals & candles
  • Welcome drinks on terrace
  • Romantic fireplace dinner at hotel; overnight
Day 2: Private Sightseeing — Mall Road, Jakhu Temple, Sunset Point & Rooftop Dinner

Spend the day exploring Shimla's iconic sights in the privacy of a couple-only cab. Visit Jakhu Temple (2,455 m) — the forest path to the temple, lined with pine trees and cheeky monkeys, is a delightful experience. Walk along The Ridge for panoramic mountain photography — the snow-capped peaks visible on clear days make for dramatic backdrop shots. Visit Christ Church, Scandal Point, and Viceregal Lodge at your own pace. In the evening, watch the sunset from Prospect Hill or another scenic viewpoint, followed by a private candlelight dinner on your hotel's rooftop with twinkling city lights below.

  • Private couple sightseeing by AC cab
  • Jakhu Temple — forest path & monkeys
  • The Ridge, Christ Church & Scandal Point photography
  • Viceregal Lodge (IIAS)
  • Sunset point photography
  • Rooftop candlelight dinner; overnight
Day 3: Naldehra Golf Course & Kufri Horse Riding — Romance in Pine Forests

Drive to Naldehra (23 km from Shimla) to visit Asia's oldest golf course — a beautifully maintained 9-hole course at 2,200 m surrounded by towering deodar trees. Non-golfers can stroll the fairways and enjoy the scenic tranquility; golfers can play a round (fees not included). Continue to Kufri (2,622 m) where you'll ride horses together through dense pine forests to reach elevated viewpoints — the combination of pine-scented mountain air, horse riding, and valley views is wonderfully romantic. Visit the Wildflower Hall viewpoint for spectacular panoramas. Return to Shimla for a private couples tea time arranged at a charming cafe.

  • Drive to Naldehra — Asia's oldest golf course (1905)
  • Stroll or play on the 9-hole mountain golf course (fees extra)
  • Kufri — couples horse riding through pine forests
  • Wildflower Hall viewpoint — spectacular panorama
  • Couples private tea at heritage cafe; overnight
Day 4: Shopping & Departure

Enjoy a final leisurely breakfast at the heritage hotel. Shimla's markets offer some of the best Himalayan souvenirs — handwoven Himachali shawls in distinctive geometric patterns, Angori wine, local apple jams and squash, and beautifully crafted wooden items from Lakkar Bazaar. Your return Volvo to Delhi departs in the morning.

  • Final breakfast at heritage hotel
  • Shopping — Himachali shawls, apple products, Lakkar Bazaar
  • Board return AC Volvo to Delhi
