Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Shimla — Evening Mall Road Walk

Board your AC Volvo from Delhi in the evening. Arrive in Shimla by morning and check into your hotel on or near Mall Road. After freshening up and breakfast, spend the afternoon at leisure on Mall Road — the iconic pedestrian promenade lined with Victorian-era stone buildings, shops, and cafes that make Shimla's colonial character so distinctive. Try the local apple juice, kachoris, and if the weather is clear, enjoy excellent mountain views from Ridge Road.

  • Board AC Volvo Delhi (evening)
  • Arrive Shimla morning; hotel check-in
  • Afternoon leisure on Mall Road
  • Evening dinner on Ridge Road
  • Overnight
Day 2: Shimla Full Sightseeing — Jakhu, Ridge, Christ Church & Viceregal Lodge

Shimla's classic sights day. Start at Jakhu Temple (2,455 m) — a sacred Hanuman temple at the summit of Jakhu Hill, reached by a forest path populated by hundreds of monkeys. The 108-foot Hanuman statue is visible from across the city. Walk to The Ridge for panoramic views, see the neo-Gothic Christ Church (1857), visit Scandal Point, and tour the magnificent Viceregal Lodge (now Indian Institute of Advanced Study) — a Tudor-style mansion that once served as the summer headquarters of the British Viceroy of India. End at Lakkar Bazaar for wooden handicraft shopping.

  • Jakhu Temple — sacred Hanuman shrine at 2,455 m
  • The Ridge — panoramic Himalayan panorama
  • Christ Church (1857) & Scandal Point
  • Viceregal Lodge — Tudor heritage mansion
  • Lakkar Bazaar — wooden crafts
  • Overnight
Day 3: Kufri — Snow Activities, Himalayan Nature Park & Horse Riding

Drive to Kufri (2,622 m), 16 km from Shimla. Kufri Fun World offers seasonal skiing and sledding (extra charges apply). The Himalayan Nature Park is home to Himalayan brown bears, leopards, Siberian huskies, and colourful pheasants. Hire horses for a scenic ride through dense pine forests to panoramic viewpoints above the valley — the combination of pine-scented air, mountain views, and horse riding is uniquely Himalayan. Visit the Chini Bungalow viewpoint before returning to Shimla.

  • Drive to Kufri (16 km, altitude 2,622 m)
  • Kufri Fun World — skiing/sledding in season
  • Himalayan Nature Park — bears, leopards & pheasants
  • Horse riding through pine forests
  • Chini Bungalow viewpoint
  • Return to Shimla; overnight
Day 4: Chail — Royal Palace, World's Highest Cricket Ground & Deodar Forest

Drive 45 km from Shimla to Chail — a hidden gem at 2,250 m, built by the Maharaja of Patiala in 1891 after he was supposedly banished from Shimla by the British Viceroy. Visit the historic Chail Palace, now a heritage hotel, with its beautiful architecture and spectacular valley views. See the famous Chail Cricket Ground — at 2,444 m, it is believed to be the highest cricket ground in the world, built by the Maharaja for his own team. Visit the Chail Military School campus and the Kali Tibba temple. Walk through the magnificent deodar forest surrounding Chail — some trees here are over 150 years old. Return to Shimla for the last night.

  • Drive to Chail (45 km from Shimla, altitude 2,250 m)
  • Chail Palace — 1891 royal heritage architecture
  • Chail Cricket Ground — world's highest (2,444 m)
  • Chail Military School campus visit
  • Kali Tibba temple & ancient deodar forest walk
  • Return to Shimla; overnight
Day 5: Shopping & Departure to Delhi

Final breakfast at the hotel. Browse Mall Road's shops for Himachali woolens, apple products, and handicrafts. Your return Volvo departs Shimla in the morning, arriving Delhi by evening.

  • Final breakfast and check-out
  • Mall Road shopping — woolens, apple products
  • Board return AC Volvo to Delhi
